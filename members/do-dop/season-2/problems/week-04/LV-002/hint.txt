Implementation Hints for LV-002: Spot Price Optimization

1. Price Comparison Strategy:
   - Fetch spot prices for the same instance type across all target regions/AZs
   - Calculate discount percentage: (on_demand - spot) / on_demand * 100
   - Track price stability over 30 days (low variance = more stable)

2. Interruption Rate Estimation:
   - Sudden price spikes (>2x in 15 min) indicate capacity reclamation
   - Count frequency of spikes per AZ/instance-type as proxy for interruption rate
   - AWS doesn't expose interruption rate directly; infer from price volatility

3. Scoring Algorithm:
   - Score = w1 * savings_pct + w2 * stability_score - w3 * interruption_risk
   - Normalize each factor to 0-1 range before weighting
   - Suggest default weights: savings=0.4, stability=0.3, risk=0.3

4. Fleet Diversification:
   - Recommend 3+ instance families with similar vCPU/memory ratios
   - Spread across 2+ AZs to reduce correlated interruptions
   - Include fallback to On-Demand for critical baseline capacity

5. Output Format:
   - Rank regions by composite score
   - Show savings vs On-Demand in dollar terms (monthly projection)
   - Include confidence level based on data points available
